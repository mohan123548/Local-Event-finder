2. Proto-Personas 

👤 Persona 1: Event Seeker

Name: Rahul Sharma
Age: 22
Background: College student studying engineering, recently moved to a new city
Technical Skill: Moderate (comfortable with apps and websites)

Rahul is a university student who recently relocated for his studies and is looking to build a social and professional network. He spends a lot of time on his phone and prefers simple, fast-loading apps. He is interested in tech meetups, hackathons, and casual social events where he can meet people. Rahul often feels overwhelmed by too many irrelevant event listings on other platforms. He wants something that shows nearby and relevant events quickly. He would use this system to discover events based on his interests and location. He values ease of use and quick filtering options.

Overall summary :
   1. Techinical events .
   2. Near by locations(new city)
   3. Networking and sharing new skills .
