# # **👥 TARGET USERS**

The primary target users of the **Local Event Finder** are students, working professionals, tourists, and local residents who want to discover nearby events quickly and easily. These users often face difficulties finding reliable information about social gatherings, workshops, cultural events, and community activities because event details are spread across different platforms and social media pages.
