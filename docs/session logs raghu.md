Q1. Which concept (personas / scenarios / user stories / features) was most useful for your project, and why?

The most useful concept for our project was personas and user stories. Personas helped us understand different users like event seekers, organizers, and admins, along with their needs and problems. User stories helped us design useful features such as searching events, location filters, notifications, and personalized recommendations in a simple and user-friendly way.      

Q2. What is one assumption about your users that this workshop forced you to challenge?

At first, we assumed that users would prefer many advanced filters and detailed features. However, we learned that most users want a simple, fast, and easy-to-use platform. This workshop helped us focus more on usability and reducing complexity in the interface.
